Bangladesh

Bangladesh is a beautiful country in South Asia, known for its rich culture, natural beauty, and friendly people. It is home to the world’s largest mangrove forest, the Sundarbans, and the longest sea beach in Cox’s Bazar. The country has a tropical climate with lush green fields and rivers flowing across its land. Agriculture, especially rice and jute farming, plays a major role in the economy. Bangladesh is also famous for its vibrant festivals, delicious food, and traditional crafts. Despite challenges, the nation is growing rapidly and making progress in education, technology, and industry.
